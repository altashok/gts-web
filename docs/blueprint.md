# **App Name**: Tamil Wisdom Hub

## Core Features:

- Informational Pages: Static pages for About Us, Team, Activities, and Affiliations, populated with content adapted from globaltamilschool.co.uk.
- Hero Image Carousel: Rotating carousel on the homepage to showcase key images and highlights.
- WhatsApp Floating Icon: A persistent WhatsApp icon linking to the school's WhatsApp contact.
- Course Enrollment Form: A form to collect enrollment information and send an email to globaltamilschool@gmail.com.
- Contact Form: A form for users to send inquiries and messages to the school.
- Footer Links and Info: Footer section with address, copyright, and important website links for easy navigation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Golden yellow (#FECF00), echoing the user request. It symbolizes prosperity, optimism, and intellect. Because of its high saturation, this color will be tempered by desaturated colors in the rest of the palette.
- Background color: Very light warm gray (#F5F5F0), a heavily desaturated, darkened version of the primary hue. This will complement the school's identity and focus on academic knowledge, create visual comfort, and make other elements stand out.
- Accent color: Light brown (#CDAA7D) to provide an earthy tone and reinforce an aura of history and tradition.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif offering a clean and accessible style that makes it highly readable and provides a modern, humanist feel for conveying information.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif with high contrast that harmonizes with the body text to offer a refined, elegant, high-end aesthetic, creating an interesting and engaging textual hierarchy.
- Use of clear, symbolic icons in a uniform style, relating to education and Tamil culture to enhance usability and understanding.
- Clean, modern layout with clear sections for each type of information, ensuring easy navigation and a pleasant browsing experience.
- Subtle animations (e.g., carousel transitions, hover effects) to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
